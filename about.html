About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore
Photographer | Traveler | Nature Enthusiast

Malcolm Lismore is a seasoned photographer based in the heart of Scotland, specializing in landscape, wildlife, and event photography. With over a decade of experience behind the lens, Malcolm has developed a unique style that combines technical skill with an artistic eye, capturing the natural beauty of Scotland’s diverse scenery and its wildlife.

His journey into photography began during a hiking trip in the Scottish Highlands, where he fell in love with the landscape and its unspoiled beauty. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a full-fledged career, and since then, Malcolm has traveled across Scotland, capturing breathtaking landscapes and wildlife in every corner of the country.

Malcolm’s passion for photography is not just about taking pictures – it’s about telling stories. Each of his photographs speaks of a moment in time, a fleeting glimpse of nature’s grandeur or an intimate connection with wildlife. His work is about sharing his love for nature with others, inspiring them to see the world through his eyes and appreciate its beauty.

Throughout his career, Malcolm has had the privilege of working with various clients, from local businesses to international brands, providing stunning visuals for marketing campaigns, exhibitions, and publications. His portfolio is a reflection of his commitment to excellence and his deep respect for the natural world.

"Photography is not just a passion, it's a way of seeing the world in all its beauty and complexity." – Malcolm Lismore
